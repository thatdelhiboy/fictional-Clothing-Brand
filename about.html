The Wrightwood Story

Hello. Welcome to Mens store. So glad you’re here.

We’re Doug & Mike, the father-son founders of Men's Store. We’ve been working in the Fation industry for pretty much our entire lives. We used to be in the wholesale business, but now we’re designing, sourcing, and buying unique fation from around the world and selling it directly to you.

The big chains used to be our clients. Now they’re our competitors. And we’ve learned a few things about them over the years. For example, the big chains don’t charge high prices because they want lots of money. Or at least that’s not the only reason.
